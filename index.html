MSc Research

My research area of interest involves studying the effects of recreational substances on the mind and body, as well as advocating for policies that promote their safe usage. I foresee a world in which individuals may choose to use decriminalized and regulated recreational drugs comfortably, with full understanding of their effects and recommended dosages to minimize harm to both their person as well as to society.
