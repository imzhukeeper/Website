Master's Research

My research area of interest involves applying precision medicine towards drug dosing regimen for personalizing medical treatments.

By incorporating genomic, proteomic, and metabolomic data into pharmacology models, we can better understand the interindividual differences involved in the metabolism of various drugs, and ultimately provide better predictions for optimizing medical care.
